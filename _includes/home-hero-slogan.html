Datensummit is going to be prospective and retrospective at the same time. For the last five years the Open Knowledge Foundation Germany has been advocating open knowledge, open data, transparency, and civil participation in Germany. Now we want to use the Datensummit as an opportunity to invite everyone who wants to advance open data in Germany.

Nun wollen wir mit allen feiern, die Open Data in Deutschland voranbringen, weiterdenken oder kennenlernen möchten. Setzt die Partyhüte auf und springt zum Anmeldeformular.
